About Us

Who Are We

If this is the first time you have heard about us or the first time you have visited our site. you might be wondering who we are. We are a bunch of young and hungry individuals who love to accept challenges and thrive on them. We are hard working and completely dedicated to our work.Built in 2009, this was the dream project of one of our founding members and the current chairman of this company: Manish Pokharel.However,the success behind this production company goes to all the individuals who have helped in the growth of this company. We thank our customers and staff members that have helped us achieve the goals which we thought was never possible.

What Do We Do

We especialize in customer service. That is our top priority. Our talented staff produces music for artists who are talented but cannot afford to spend money. We also promote such artists' music and produce their music videos. Our company also hosts events and parties.
